Events

Each team will be scheduled a time throughout the day to participate in 8 events. Scenarios will last approximately 20 minutes. To be eligible for the overall winning trophy, teams must complete all scheduled events.

Suspicious Situation

An Explorer team will be dispatched to investigate a reported suspicious vehicle.

Active Assailant

An Explorer team will be dispatched to the scene of an active shooter with multiple casualties.

Officer Needs Assistance

An Explorer team will be called upon to aid an officer in duress.

Exigent Entry

An Explorer team will be presented with a situation and must make a decision to enter a structure and use force.

Unknown Trouble

An Explorer team will be summoned to resolve a disturbance involving multiple individuals.

Crime Scene Investigation

An Explorer team will be presented with a crime scene and will process it using conventional techniques.

Burglary In Progress

An Explorer team will be sent to investigate an active burglary.

Vehicle Ambush

While on patrol, an Explorer team will come upon enemy fire and must mitigate the situation.

Additional Events

These events are elective and will not be considered for the overall winning trophy. Posts may choose to compete in one or both of the additional events

Pistol Qualification

Two Explorers per registered team may participate in a live-fire qualification. They will shoot 30 rounds at varying distances using a handgun which will be provided.

Honor Guard Performance

An honor guard team will display their proficiency in basic techniques including Inspection, Colors, Casket Watch and Flag Fold. Additional info will be provided to participants. The winning team will present colors at the award ceremony.

SCORING CRITERIA

Regardless of agency or jurisdiction, Explorer teams will be judged according to a grading rubric which focuses on a wide set of generally accepted law enforcement procedures.

To ensure success, teams should receive the following training prior to arrival at competition:

working as a team
decision making skills
observation & recall skills
communication techniques

With Suspects, Dispatch and Partners

basic patrol tactics
general police procedures
use of contact & cover concepts
overall officer safety techniques

Base Camp

The competition site offers ample space for teams to set up an optional base camp for the day. A designated area will be available for RVs, trailers & shade tents.

Note that overnight camping is not permitted.
